2Equilibrium

Holistic Wellness Journey App

Core Concept: A 30-day wellness journey app that guides users through daily lessons, journaling, and gentle movement. Content unlocks one day at a time based on when the user created their account.

1 Authentication

  • Sign Up / Sign In - Email and password authentication via Supabase
  • Profile Creation - Automatically creates user profile on signup
  • Session Persistence - Stays logged in between app sessions

2 Today Screen (Home Tab)

  • Day Counter - Shows current day (e.g., "Day 2") based on account creation date
  • Progress Ring - Visual progress through the 30-day journey
  • Current Streak - Tracks consecutive days of lesson completion
  • Today's Lesson Card - Shows the lesson for today with "Begin" button
  • Completed State - After completing the lesson, shows "Great work today!" with option to review
  • Daily Affirmation - Inspirational quote that changes with each day's lesson
  • Quick Actions - Shortcuts to Journal, Journey Map, and Water tracking

3 Lesson Screen

Content Journal Movement

Content Tab

  • Lesson title and introduction
  • Main educational content
  • Key takeaways (bullet points with checkmarks)
  • Today's Action step (highlighted card)
  • Daily Affirmation
  • Nourishment Tip

Journal Tab

  • Three prompts with direct input fields:
    • Today's Reflection (sun icon)
    • Deeper Reflection (lightbulb icon)
    • Gratitude (heart icon)
  • Save Journal Entry button
  • Read-only view after saving (one entry per lesson)
  • Shows "Journal entry completed" banner when done

Movement Tab

  • Basic movement suggestion
  • Personalized movement suggestion
  • "Mark Complete" button for tracking
  • Shows "Completed" state after marking done
Complete Lesson Button:
  • Disabled until journal prompts are answered
  • Shows "Complete Journal First" if journal not done
  • Marks lesson complete and returns to home

4 Journey Screen (Journey Tab)

  • Phase Progress - Shows "Phase 1: Foundation" with progress ring
  • Chapter List - 6 chapters, each with 5 daily lessons
  • Chapter Cards - Show progress, lesson count, and completion status
  • Continue Button - Opens chapter detail

The 6 Chapters

  1. Awakening Your Wellness Path (Days 1-5)
  2. Nourishment Fundamentals (Days 6-10)
  3. Mindful Eating Rituals (Days 11-15)
  4. Meal Architecture (Days 16-20)
  5. Gentle Movement Foundations (Days 21-25)
  6. Mindset & Self-Compassion (Days 26-30)

5 Chapter Detail Screen

  • Chapter Header - Color-coded with chapter badge, title, subtitle
  • Progress Ring - Shows lessons completed out of total
  • Lesson List - All 5 lessons with:
    • Day number indicator
    • Completed checkmark (green) for finished lessons
    • Lock icon for future lessons
    • Tap to open accessible lessons
  • Chapter Quiz Section - Locked until all 5 lessons complete
  • Next Chapter Card - Appears after quiz passed

6 Quiz System

  • 7-8 questions per chapter - Mix of multiple choice, true/false, reflection
  • 70% passing threshold - Must score 70%+ to pass

Pass Result

Celebration modal with confetti animation, unlocks next chapter

Fail Result

Shows missed topics, 24-hour wait to retry

  • Review missed topics - Can review content for failed questions

7 Journal Screen (Journal Tab)

  • Daily Wellness Check:
    • Mood selector (5 emoji options)
    • Energy level selector (5 levels)
    • Water intake tracker (glasses)
    • Free-form notes field
  • One entry per day - Form becomes read-only after saving
  • Today's date display - Shows current date at top

9 Day Unlock System

  • Calendar-based unlocking - New day unlocks at midnight local time
  • Based on account creation - Day 1 = signup date, Day 2 = next calendar day
  • Maximum Day 30 - Phase 1 caps at 30 days
  • Future lessons locked - Shows lock icon, not tappable

10 Progress Tracking (Supabase)

  • completed_lessons - Array of lesson IDs marked complete
  • current_day - Highest unlocked day
  • current_streak - Consecutive completion days
  • longest_streak - Best streak achieved
  • subscription_start_date - Used for day calculations
  • badges - Achievement badges earned

11 Data Persistence

  • Supabase Database - All progress synced to cloud
  • Zustand Stores - Local state management:
    • userStore - Auth, profile, subscription
    • progressStore - Lessons, streaks, quizzes
    • journalStore - Journal entries
  • AsyncStorage - Caches auth state locally
